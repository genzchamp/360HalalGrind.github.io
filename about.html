About Nasiruddeen “Oba” Abdulkarim

Bismillah. My name is Nasiruddeen Abdulkarim. Born Muslim (Alhamdulillah), a proud Yoruba son — in my neighborhood they call me Oba.

My Journey

I grew up curious about technology, sports, and how the modern world works. I played football since childhood and began basketball in early 2023. I studied at the University of Jos (Department of Agricultural Economics & Extension) — I learnt Poultry farming and consultation since 2014-2023. Life gave me trials: I lost both my parents young. These experiences shaped my heart, my focus, and my faith.

Why 360HalalGrind?

360HalalGrind is my answer: a place where faith meets the hustle. Here, I share the steps I used to create my first website using ChatGPT, free tools, and determination. My goal is to help others build halal wealth with integrity.

What I Believe

GoodLife is built on halal principles — success that is permissible and blessed. “Living only with The Permission of The Unique ONE” is a reminder that everything we do is by Allah’s will.
